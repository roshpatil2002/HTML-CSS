About This Course

Amazon Web Services (AWS) stands as a titan in the realm of cloud computing, offering a vast array of services and solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses and developers worldwide. Established by Amazon in 2006, AWS has since evolved into a comprehensive platform encompassing computing power, storage options, networking capabilities, databases, machine learning tools, and more. Its scalability, reliability, and flexibility have made it the go-to choice for startups, enterprises, and government agencies alike, enabling them to innovate swiftly and operate with efficiency. With a global network of data centers, AWS ensures low latency and high availability, bolstering the performance of applications and services hosted on its infrastructure. Furthermore, AWS's commitment to security and compliance standards provides customers with peace of mind, knowing their data is safeguarded against threats. As cloud computing continues to reshape the technological landscape, AWS remains at the forefront, empowering organizations to transform their operations and achieve new heights of success.

Course's objective

Amazon Web Services (AWS) has a multifaceted approach to its objectives, encompassing innovation, scalability, customer satisfaction, and market dominance. At its core, AWS aims to continually innovate and expand its cloud computing services, offering a comprehensive suite of solutions that cater to diverse needs across industries. Scalability is a key focus, with AWS striving to provide flexible and reliable infrastructure that can seamlessly accommodate the evolving demands of businesses, from startups to enterprises. Customer satisfaction is paramount, with AWS prioritizing exceptional service delivery, robust security, and ongoing support to ensure clients derive maximum value from their cloud investments. Additionally, AWS seeks to maintain its position as a leader in the cloud market, continuously refining its offerings, expanding its global footprint, and outpacing competitors through technological advancements and strategic partnerships. Overall, AWS is dedicated to empowering businesses worldwide with the tools and resources needed to thrive in the digital era.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understanding AWS Services: A comprehensive understanding of the various services offered by AWS, including computing, storage, databases, networking, analytics, machine learning, and more.
  • AWS Certification Preparation: Many AWS courses are designed to prepare individuals for AWS certification exams such as AWS Certified Solutions Architect, AWS Certified Developer, AWS Certified SysOps Administrator, etc. Achieving certification validates your AWS expertise to potential employers.
  • Hands-On Experience: Practical experience in deploying, managing, and troubleshooting applications and infrastructure on AWS. Hands-on labs or projects may be included in the course to reinforce theoretical concepts.
  • Scalability and Elasticity: Learning how to design and deploy applications on AWS that can scale seamlessly based on demand, leveraging services like Auto Scaling, Elastic Load Balancing, and AWS Lambda.
  • Security Best Practices: Understanding AWS security best practices, including identity and access management (IAM), encryption, network security, and compliance.

Curriculum refers to the entirety of the organized experience of students in any institutional setting towards educational aims and objectives. The elements that constitute and bring to life a Curriculum are numerous, and include goals and objectives, syllabi, content to be taught and learnt, pedagogical practices and assessment, teacing-learning materials, school and classroom practices, learning environment and culture of the institution, and more. There are other matters that directly affect a Curriculum and its practice or are integrally related while not being within the Curriculum. These include the Teachers and their capacities, the involvement of parents and communities, issues of access to institutions, resources available, administrative and support structures, and more.

The Curricula across our country must be informed by and be fully responsive to the glorious unity and diversity of India. The imagination of NEP 2020, where institutions and educators are highly empowered - including to develop Curricula - is energized by this diversity and the nurturing of it. States have the Constitutional mandate to provide high-quality education to all children, and their own unique State contexts inform their own approaches to Curricula. A Curriculum Framework must support exactly that - it is a framework to help develop all the diverse Curricula in the country, while enabling consonances and harmony across the country and providing a basis for quality and equity.

Thus, a Curriculum Framework provides the guiding principles, goals, structure, and elements for the development of Curricula, informed by which the syllabi, teaching-learning-materials including play materials, workbooks, and textbooks, and assessment methods will be developed by the relevant functionaries, including Teachers, in the States, Boards, and schools.
